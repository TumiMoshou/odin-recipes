Beef Taco

Description

This is an easy beef taco recipe. Using prefectly seasoned, tender ground beef is always a good idea.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Warm up the tortillas and prepare all the ingredients and toppings
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat
  3. Add in the seasoning mix and cook until the meat is cooked through
  4. Assemble the tacos and enjoy
