Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Bolognese sauce is an Italian dish made up of a meat-based tomato sauce served with spaghetti. This spaghetti with bolognese is not only easy to make but it is also very delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 28 ounces of crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
  2. Add the onions and saute until lightly golden
  3. Add the garlic and fry for 1 minute
  4. Add the ground beef and fry until fully cooked
  5. Add the tomato paste and mix it with the meat
  6. Add the oregano, tomatoes, parsley and season with salt and pepper
  7. Simmer for 15 minutes. Serve with freshly cooked spaghetti and enjoy
