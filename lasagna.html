Lasagna

Description

This is a classic homemade lasagna recipe with layers of melted mozzarella cheese, parmesean ricotta cheese and tomato meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 3/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Start by making homemade meat sauce
  2. Make a parmesan ricotta cheese mixture
  3. Use your favourite lasagna noodles
  4. Add some cheese
  5. Bake covered for 15 minutes
